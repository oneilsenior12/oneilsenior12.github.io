Company Overview

Wholesale built for clarity and speed.

We operate as a nondurable goods merchant wholesaler with an emphasis on short lead times, transparent documentation, and dependable replenishment cycles. Our partners include regional distributors, grocery groups, independent chains, and e-commerce operators that require reliable flow without single-brand dependency.

Stock positions and transit ETAs are updated daily based on carrier scans and hub feedback. Every item we move ships with a digital pack that includes lot codes, safety data/spec sheets, country-of-origin records, and carton dimensions for receiving teams.

From Port Saint Lucie we serve the Southeast and beyond using cross-dock partners. We keep the conversation simple: confirm need, verify compliance, ship clean, and close with a short after-action file.
